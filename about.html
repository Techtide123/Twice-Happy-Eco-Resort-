About Us

Welcome To Our Twice Happy Eco Hotel & Resort

Welcome to Our Twice Happy Eco Hotel & Resort, located at Khata No: 335/1071, Plot No: 1936/2865, Chandragiri, Gajapati, Odisha. Our resort offers a harmonious blend of comfort and nature, providing guests with a peaceful and eco-friendly stay experience. We feature a variety of well-appointed rooms, including the King Suit Room, Premium Suit Room, and Standard Room. All accommodations are available under the EP (European Plan) rate plan, with additional options such as CP, MAP, and AP available to suit your needs.
