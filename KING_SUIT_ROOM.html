Executive Room

The King Suite Room offers an exceptional blend of luxury, comfort, and style. Elegantly designed and thoughtfully furnished, each suite features a spacious layout with a plush king-size bed, modern decor, and premium amenities to ensure a truly relaxing stay. With four rooms available, the King Suite accommodates a base occupancy of two adults and a maximum of three guests.
The European Plan (EP) rate includes accommodation only, allowing guests the flexibility to explore various dining options at their leisure. The sell price is ₹3,800 during the season and ₹3,300 in the off-season, with an extra adult charge of ₹500. Guests can indulge in a remarkable culinary experience at the hotel’s on-site restaurants, where skilled chefs prepare delectable dishes using the freshest local ingredients. From a sumptuous breakfast to a leisurely lunch and an exquisite dinner, every meal is served in an elegant and inviting ambiance, promising a memorable dining experience throughout your stay.
