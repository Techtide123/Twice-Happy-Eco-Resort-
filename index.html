Welcome To Our Twice Happy Eco Hotel & Resort

Welcome to Happy Eco, where luxury meets comfort in the heart of Gajapati. Since 2025, we have been dedicated to providing an exceptional stay for our guests, blending modern amenities with timeless elegance.Our beautifully designed rooms and suites offer stunning views and plush accommodations, ensuring a restful retreat whether you're here for business or leisure.
